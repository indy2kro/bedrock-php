    Bedrock PHP¶

    Adoption & Representation License¶

    Purpose of This Document¶

    This document defines the terms under which individuals, teams, and organizations may formally adopt, represent adherence to, and integrate Bedrock PHP into their projects.

    Its goals are to:

    • keep Bedrock PHP publicly readable and accessible
    • provide a clear, tangible right when a license is purchased
    • distinguish between informal use and formal adoption
    • create a sustainable funding model for the maintenance of Bedrock PHP

    This document is normative.

    1. Reading vs Adoption¶

    1.1 Reading and Informal Use¶

    Anyone may, without license:

    • read Bedrock PHP documents
    • download Bedrock PHP documents
    • learn from or be inspired by Bedrock PHP
    • apply ideas privately or experimentally
    • reference Bedrock PHP casually in discussion

    No permission or payment is required for these activities.

    1.2 Formal Adoption and Representation¶

    A license is required to:

    • state that a project, team, or organization adheres to Bedrock PHP
    • treat Bedrock PHP as an internal or external standard
    • pin a specific Bedrock PHP version as a baseline
    • include Bedrock PHP in onboarding, policy, or compliance material
    • represent alignment with Bedrock PHP to stakeholders, clients, or auditors

    Formal adoption is an organizational act, not a personal belief.

    2. What the License Grants¶

    A valid Adoption & Representation License grants the right to:

    • formally adopt a specific Bedrock PHP release
    • state "We follow Bedrock PHP vX.Y" internally or externally
    • redistribute the licensed release internally
    • train teams against the licensed release
    • retain the licensed release for historical reference

    The license does not grant:

    • certification or approval
    • endorsement by the Bedrock PHP authors
    • immunity from deviation
    • permission to misrepresent compliance

    3. License Scope and Tiers¶

    Licenses are scoped by who is allowed to represent adoption.

    3.1 Individual License¶

    Grants:

    • formal adoption for personal projects
    • use in learning, teaching, or evaluation
    • reference during consulting work

    Restrictions:

    • does not allow representing client or employer adherence

    3.2 Team License¶

    Grants:

    • formal adoption by a single team or product
    • internal redistribution within that team
    • inclusion in team documentation and onboarding

    Restrictions:

    • does not extend to unrelated teams or products

    3.3 Organization License¶

    Grants:

    • formal adoption across an entire organization
    • internal redistribution without team-level restriction
    • inclusion in company-wide standards

    Restrictions:

    • limited to a single legal entity
    • does not permit external redistribution

    4. Versioning and Duration¶

    Licenses are issued per Bedrock PHP release.

    • Each release is frozen and versioned
    • A license applies to the specific release it references
    • Organizations may continue using a licensed release indefinitely

    A new release requires:

    • an explicit decision to adopt
    • a new license

    Licenses do not imply:

    • automatic upgrades
    • continuous updates
    • evergreen maintenance

    5. Deviation and Honesty¶

    Licensed adopters may deviate from Bedrock PHP.

    However:

    • deviations must be documented internally
    • representation must remain honest
    • partial or selective adoption must not be misrepresented as full adherence

    The license grants the right to represent alignment, not perfection.

    6. Prohibited Uses¶

    Without explicit permission, license holders must not:

    • claim certification or endorsement
    • use Bedrock PHP branding to imply approval
    • resell or sublicense the standard
    • incorporate Bedrock PHP into competing standards

    7. Enforcement Philosophy¶

    This license is enforced through:

    • clarity, not surveillance
    • good faith, not audits
    • alignment, not punishment

    Bedrock PHP relies on professional integrity, not legal intimidation.

    8. Relationship to Profiles¶

    This license governs adoption of the Bedrock PHP core standard.

    Profiles:

    • are optional
    • are licensed separately
    • may require additional licenses for commercial use

    Adopting Bedrock PHP does not require using any profile.

    Closing Statement¶

    Bedrock PHP is free to read so that it can remain universal.

    Licensing exists to make formal adoption explicit, durable, and sustainable.

    If this license becomes a source of friction or fear, it has failed its purpose.
